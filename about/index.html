Vision

To help and train UT Dallas students to be successful Product Managers that are effective contributors to the company.

Who We Are

The Product Base is a platform provided for the UTD students to develop a deeper understanding and gain hands-on experience in Product Management. We are a diverse group that ranges from freshers to developers who have worked at various levels of an organization. We come from different academic backgrounds and are not limited to Information Technology and Management, Computer Science, and Business Analytics degree programs.

Product Management is a niche field, with the ropes better learned by experience. We learn through various activities, expert sessions, workshops, and case studies.
